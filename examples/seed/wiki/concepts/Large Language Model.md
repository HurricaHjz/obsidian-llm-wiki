## Definition
A **Large Language Model (LLM)** is a Transformer-based neural network trained on large text corpora to
predict and generate text, used for reasoning, dialogue, and many language tasks. *(Example seed page —
safe to delete.)*

## Key Points
- LLMs are compared on benchmarks such as [[MMLU]].
- [[GPT]] is one family of LLMs, made by [[OpenAI]].
